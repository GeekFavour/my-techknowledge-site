# About

Hey, I’m Bonifas 👋

I work as an Infrastructure and Platform Engineer, and I spend most of my time around cloud, servers, networking, security, and troubleshooting all kinds of things in between.

This site is my personal technical knowledge base — a place where I document things I learn while working, building, breaking, fixing, and figuring things out.

A lot of what you’ll find here comes from day-to-day real-world experience: troubleshooting production issues, testing configurations, writing scripts, solving weird infrastructure problems, and making notes so I don’t have to learn the same lesson twice.

I originally started keeping these notes for myself — mainly so I’d always have a place to come back to whenever I hit the same issue again. Over time I realized these notes could also be useful to others working on similar things, so I decided to put them all here in one place.

You’ll find topics around Azure, Cloudflare, Linux, DevOps, servers, networking, DNS, SSL, security, monitoring, automation, and general infrastructure operations — along with practical fixes, how-to guides, configs, and troubleshooting steps.

My goal is simple: document what I learn, keep improving along the way, and hopefully make someone else’s troubleshooting a little easier too.

If you found this site while trying to fix something — I hope you find what you’re looking for here 🙂
